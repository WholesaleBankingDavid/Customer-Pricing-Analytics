# Use Case Vision

## RM Deal Prioritization & Pricing Guidance

The MVP supports Relationship Managers, pricing teams, and sales steering teams in prioritizing active commercial-loan deals and interpreting pricing guidance at facility level.

## Target Users

- Relationship Managers who manage active negotiations and client dialogue.
- Pricing teams that review margins, fees, and internal guardrails.
- Sales steering teams that monitor pipeline quality and economic attractiveness.

## Core Questions

- Which deals should the RM prioritize?
- How likely is a commercial close?
- Which deals are economically attractive?
- Where is the price or margin corridor?
- Which facility supports or weakens the overall deal?

## Outputs

- Deal Priority
- Win Probability
- Expected Deal Value
- Pricing Zone: `Red`, `Guardrail`, `Recommended`, `Stretch`
- Facility-level guardrails and key drivers

## Decision Support Boundary

The tool provides pricing guidance and prioritization signals. It does not set prices automatically and does not use competitor data as a decision input.
